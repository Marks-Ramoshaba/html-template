Practical Guidelines for

Novice Teachers

The day-to-day complexities of teaching in South African classrooms are particularly challenging for novice teachers. Although equipped with knowledge and competencies from their years of study, often there is a gap between what new teachers know and how to apply that knowledge in a ‘real’ classroom.

  • Teaching as a career in South Africa — what does it really entail?
  • You as a new professional — what does this mean, how does it change your identity and how do you look after yourself.
  • Diverse learners — how to deal with differing learning styles, temperaments and behaviours in one classroom.
  • Classroom matters — from surviving the 1st day to making your own inexpensive resources.
  • Extracurricular activities — from coaching to organising a school function.
